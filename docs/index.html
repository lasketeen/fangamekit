What is Lask's Fangame Kit?
Lask's Fangame Kit (otherwise known as LFK) is a kit made for EToH Fangames filled with various features and options for fangame builders to use to create their fangames while giving them the best building blocks to begin with.

What are key qualities and features the kit has?
The kit comes with much better optimizations, instructions for nearly everything, fangame settings for countless features, all-jump and practice gamemodes, supports both kit v6.1 and v5.5 in the same kit, and so much more! (and there will be more features to come in the future!)

Whats the charge to purchase the kit?
This kit is COMPLETELY FREE and will forever remain free of charge. Feel free to download and use the kit to your desire. No proper crediting is required, but always appreciated! I just want to make fangame development easier and better for everyone.
